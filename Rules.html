How to play sequencium

The sequencium board is a 6 by 6 board. Each square can be either blue, red, or neutral. On blue's turn, blue can click a neutral square adjacent to a blue square. The neutral square will become blue, and filled in with the highest number of its neighboring squares, plus one. Else, blue can pass. Red moves similarly. At the end of the game, the color with the highest number wins (ties are allowed).
